# Progress & Mastery

## My Progress Page (Students)
- Students can view a general progress summary from the navigation menu.
- Shows: number of active courses, completed courses, total tests taken, recent activity.
- From here, students can navigate to the detailed progress page for each course.

## Course Progress Page
- Shows detailed progress for a specific student in a specific course.
- Accessible by: the student themselves, the teacher of the course, and the organization admin.
- Shows: content completion percentage, number of stable tests completed, average score on stable tests, number of AI-generated tests taken, attempt history for that course.
- Students can open any past attempt to review their answers.

## Topic Mastery
- Each topic has a mastery score (0.0 to 1.0) calculated by the AI module based on test results.
- Mastery is updated after each submitted test attempt.
- Higher mastery means the student has demonstrated strong understanding of that topic.

## Student Dashboard
- The student's main page is the Courses Page, not a separate dashboard.
- Progress is tracked per course and per topic.

## Common Issues
- Progress not updating: make sure resources are opened and tests are submitted (not just saved).
- Mastery score not changing: mastery updates only after submitting a test, not during.
- Progress missing for a course: you need to open at least one resource or submit at least one test in that course.
- Can't see progress for a course: make sure the course is assigned to you or is public.
